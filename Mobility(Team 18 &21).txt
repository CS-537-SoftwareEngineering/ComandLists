Commands in chronological order

1. Rover Start
    -MBLTY_POW_ON
2. Decide the turning direction
    Turn Left
      - MBLTY_TURNLEFT
    Turn Right
     -MBLTY_TURNRIGHT
3. Decide the moving direction
    Move Forward
      -MBLTY_FWRD
    Move Backward
      -MBLTY_BCK
4. Calculate Distance
    Total Distance
      -MBLTY_TOTALDISATNCE
5. Rover Stop
      -MBLTY_POW_OFF
